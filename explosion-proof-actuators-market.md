<p><ul><li>Rotary Actuators</li><li>Linear Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The explosion-proof actuators market comprises two main types: rotary actuators and linear actuators. Rotary actuators convert energy into rotational motion, making them ideal for valve control in hazardous environments. They are often used in industries such as oil and gas, where explosion risks are present. Linear actuators, on the other hand, produce straight-line movement and are suitable for applications requiring precise positioning, such as in automated machinery and safety systems, ensuring safe and reliable operations in explosive atmospheres.</p></p>
