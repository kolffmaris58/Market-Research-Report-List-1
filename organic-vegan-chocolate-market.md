Dark chocolate, rich in cacao, is favored for its intense flavor and potential health benefits, appealing to both dark chocolate enthusiasts and health-conscious consumers. White chocolate, though dairy-free, is made from cocoa butter, sugar, and non-dairy ingredients, catering to those seeking a sweeter, creamier alternative without cacao solids.</p></p>
