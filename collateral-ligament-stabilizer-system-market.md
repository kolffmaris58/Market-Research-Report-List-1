<p><p>The Collateral Ligament Stabilizer System market is primarily segmented into two types: the Medial Collateral Ligament (MCL) stabilizers and the Lateral Collateral Ligament (LCL) stabilizers. MCL stabilizers are designed to provide support and stability to the inner knee, addressing injuries related to medial ligament damage. In contrast, LCL stabilizers focus on protecting the outer knee, enhancing stability during lateral movements. Both types are integral in orthopedic treatments, addressing specific knee ligament injuries through innovative surgical solutions and rehabilitation products.</p></p>
