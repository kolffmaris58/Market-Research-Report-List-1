<p><p>The Clinical Chemiluminescence Immunoassay Analyzer market encompasses various types, including CLIA (Chemiluminescent Immunoassay), CLEIA (Chemiluminescent Enzyme Immunoassay), and ECLIA (Electrochemiluminescent Immunoassay). CLIA utilizes chemiluminescent reactions for detecting analytes, while CLEIA enhances sensitivity through enzyme-linked reactions. ECLIA combines electrochemistry and luminescence for precise measurement. These technologies are crucial for diagnosing diseases, monitoring therapeutic drugs, and conducting research, driving growth in hospitals, laboratories, and diagnostic centers globally, fueling advancements in healthcare diagnostics.</p></p>
