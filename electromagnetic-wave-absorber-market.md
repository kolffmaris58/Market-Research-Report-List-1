<p><ul><li>Resonant absorbers</li><li>Broadband absorbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromagnetic wave absorber market is primarily categorized into resonant absorbers and broadband absorbers. Resonant absorbers are designed to efficiently absorb specific frequencies of electromagnetic waves, making them ideal for applications where precise frequency selectivity is required. In contrast, broadband absorbers are engineered to capture a wide range of frequencies, providing effective absorption across various wavelengths. These two types cater to different needs in industries such as telecommunications, defense, and consumer electronics, enhancing signal integrity and minimizing interference.</p></p>
