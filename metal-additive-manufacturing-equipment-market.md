<p><p>The Metal Additive Manufacturing Equipment market includes several key technologies. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) uses a high-powered laser to fuse metal powder layer by layer, enabling complex geometries and high-resolution structures. Electron Beam Melting (EBM) employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powder, offering fast build rates and the ability to work with high-temperature materials. Other technologies may include binder jetting and directed energy deposition, each serving unique applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and medical.</p></p>
