<p><p>Content Management System (CMS) tools are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CMS solutions offer flexibility and scalability, allowing users to access content from anywhere while minimizing IT infrastructure costs. They typically operate on a subscription model, providing automatic updates and maintenance. On-premises CMS solutions, on the other hand, are installed locally on a company’s servers, offering greater control over data and security but requiring more significant upfront investment and ongoing management.</p></p>
