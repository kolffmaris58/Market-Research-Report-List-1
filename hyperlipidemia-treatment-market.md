<p><p>The hyperlipidemia treatment market is segmented into familial and acquired types. Acquired hyperlipidemia, often influenced by lifestyle factors, obesity, or diabetes, requires a broader range of treatment options, including lifestyle modifications, dietary changes, and medications. Both segments focus on lowering lipid levels to reduce cardiovascular risks, driving innovation and investment in tailored therapies.</p></p>
