<p><ul><li>Homogeneous Catalyst</li><li>Heterogeneous Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Catalyst Powders Market is primarily divided into two types: homogeneous catalysts and heterogeneous catalysts. Homogeneous catalysts exist in the same phase as the reactants, often resulting in uniform reactions and easier mixing. They are typically more efficient at lower temperatures. In contrast, heterogeneous catalysts are in a different phase, usually solid, which allows for easier separation from products after reaction. This type is commonly used in industrial applications for processes like petroleum refining and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
