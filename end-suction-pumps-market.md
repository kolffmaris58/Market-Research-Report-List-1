<p><ul><li>Frame Mounted End Suction Pump</li><li>Close Coupled End Suction Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>End suction pumps, widely used in various industries, come in two main types: frame mounted and close coupled end suction pumps. Frame mounted end suction pumps feature a separate frame that supports the motor and pump, allowing for easier maintenance and flexibility in installation. In contrast, close coupled end suction pumps integrate the motor directly onto the pump body, saving space and reducing installation costs. Both types effectively handle liquids, offering efficient performance tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
