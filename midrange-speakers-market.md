<p><p>The midrange speakers market consists of various types, including cone, dome, and compression horn types. Cone midrange speakers utilize a cone-shaped driver for efficient sound reproduction, offering a broad frequency range. Dome midrange speakers use a dome diaphragm, providing smooth dispersion and clarity, ideal for home and studio applications. Compression horn types combine a diaphragm with a horn, delivering high sound pressure levels while minimizing distortion, commonly used in professional audio setups. Each type serves different audio performance and installation needs.</p></p>
