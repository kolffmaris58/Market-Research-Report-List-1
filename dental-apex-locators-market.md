<p><p>Dental apex locators are crucial tools used in endodontics to determine the working length of root canals. The market is primarily divided into two types: alarm type and digital readout devices. Alarm type apex locators provide auditory signals when the tip of the file reaches the apex of the tooth, offering a straightforward indication. In contrast, digital readout models display precise measurements on a screen, allowing for more accurate readings and enhanced visualization during procedures. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in root canal treatments.</p></p>
