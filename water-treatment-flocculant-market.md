<p><p>The water treatment flocculant market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic flocculants. Organic water treatment flocculants are typically derived from natural sources, such as plant extracts or biopolymers, and are prized for their environmental friendliness and biodegradability. In contrast, inorganic water treatment flocculants, commonly made from metal salts like aluminum sulfate, are highly effective at removing impurities but may require careful handling due to potential environmental concerns. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing water clarity and quality.</p></p>
