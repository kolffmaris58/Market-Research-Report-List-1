<p><p>Flocculants and coagulants are chemical agents used primarily in water treatment processes to facilitate the removal of suspended solids and impurities. Flocculants work by aggregating fine particles into larger clusters, or flocs, which can then be easily separated from the liquid. Coagulants, on the other hand, destabilize suspended particles through the neutralization of charges, leading to the formation of larger aggregates. These compounds are essential in various industries, including municipal water treatment, mining, and food processing.</p><p>The Flocculants and Coagulants Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period.
