<p><p>Activated bleaching earth is utilized in various technologies to refine oils and fats. The Wet Technology involves treating the earth with water, enhancing its adsorptive properties for better oil clarity. The Dry Technology processes the earth without moisture, optimizing efficiency by minimizing material handling and disposal. Vapour-phase Technology introduces steam in the activation phase, which improves the earth's surface area and reactivity. Each method caters to different industrial needs, balancing efficiency and cost-effectiveness in the bleaching process.</p></p>
