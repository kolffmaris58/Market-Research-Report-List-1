<p><strong>The Battery Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diode Type</li><li>Relay Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The battery isolators market consists of two primary types: diode type and relay type. Diode types utilize semiconductor diodes to prevent backflow of current between batteries, ensuring efficient power management and minimal voltage drop. They are lightweight and compact, making them ideal for various applications. Relay types, on the other hand, use electromechanical switches to separate batteries, offering a robust solution with higher current handling capabilities. This type is often preferred in heavy-duty applications where reliability and performance are critical.</p></p>
