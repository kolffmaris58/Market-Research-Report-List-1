<p><p>The Poly(methyl methacrylate) (PMMA) sheet market is primarily divided into two types: cast PMMA sheets and extruded PMMA sheets. Cast PMMA sheets are produced through a casting process, resulting in thicker sheets with superior optical clarity and impact resistance, making them ideal for applications like displays and signage. Extruded PMMA sheets, on the other hand, are manufactured by pushing molten material through a die, leading to a more uniform thickness and lower cost, commonly used in construction and automotive industries.</p></p>
