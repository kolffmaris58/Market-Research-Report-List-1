<p><p>The epoxy die attach adhesives market comprises two primary types: electrically insulative and electrically conductive adhesives. Electrically insulative adhesives are used for applications where electrical isolation is critical, providing excellent thermal stability and mechanical strength. In contrast, electrically conductive adhesives facilitate electrical connectivity between components, essential for high-performance applications in electronics. Both types are crucial in semiconductor packaging, offering tailored solutions to enhance reliability and performance in various industries, including automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
