<p><p>The Long Radar System market primarily includes Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed Radars. CW Radars transmit a continuous signal, enabling the accurate measurement of speed and distance, while being efficient for detecting moving targets. In contrast, Pulsed Radars emit short bursts of signals, allowing for long-range detection and precise mapping of objects in various environments. Both types are utilized in applications such as air traffic control, weather monitoring, and military surveillance, catering to different operational requirements.</p></p>
