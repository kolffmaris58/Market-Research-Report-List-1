<p><ul><li>Metal Belt</li><li>Metal Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) system market can be categorized into two main types: metal belt and metal chain. Metal belt CVTs utilize a flexible belt system that transmits power between two conical pulleys, providing smooth gear transitions and enhancing fuel efficiency. In contrast, metal chain CVTs employ a chain mechanism to achieve variable gear ratios, offering robust durability and superior torque handling. Both types are integral in optimizing engine performance across various automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
