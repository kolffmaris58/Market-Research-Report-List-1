<p><p>The Soundwall market is divided into two primary types: ground-mounted noise barriers and structure-mounted noise barriers. Ground-mounted noise barriers are installed alongside roads or properties to reduce noise pollution from external sources, effectively blocking sound waves. In contrast, structure-mounted noise barriers are affixed to existing buildings or structures, serving to minimize noise transmission between spaces. Both types aim to enhance acoustic comfort in urban environments, catering to residential, commercial, and industrial applications while addressing noise control needs.</p></p>
