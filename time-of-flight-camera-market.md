<p><p>The Time-of-Flight (ToF) camera market comprises primarily two types: CMOS and CCD ToF cameras. CMOS ToF cameras leverage complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, offering advantages like lower power consumption, faster frame rates, and compact designs, making them ideal for mobile devices and consumer electronics. In contrast, CCD ToF cameras utilize charge-coupled device technology, delivering superior image quality and low noise levels, often favored in industrial applications and scientific research for their high sensitivity and better performance in low-light conditions.</p></p>
