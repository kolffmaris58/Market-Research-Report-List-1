<p><strong>The Container Lined Bag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Layer Flexitank</li><li>Double Layer Flexitank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The container lined bag market comprises two primary types: single-layer flexitanks and double-layer flexitanks. Single-layer flexitanks are made of a single film, suitable for transporting non-hazardous liquids, offering a lightweight and cost-effective solution. In contrast, double-layer flexitanks feature an additional protective layer, enhancing durability and resistance to punctures, making them ideal for more sensitive or volatile liquids. Both types cater to different logistical needs in shipping and are designed to maximize efficiency in container utilization.</p></p>
