<p><strong>The Fiberoptic Blanket Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Knitting Fiberoptic Blanket</li><li>Woven Fiberoptic Blanket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberoptic blanket market comprises two primary types: knitting fiberoptic blankets and woven fiberoptic blankets. Knitting fiberoptic blankets utilize a series of interlocked yarns to create a flexible, lightweight fabric integrated with fiberoptic technology. This type typically offers enhanced comfort and adaptability. In contrast, woven fiberoptic blankets are crafted by interlacing threads in a more structured manner, providing durability and strength. Both types are designed to promote thermal regulation and can be used in various applications, including medical and therapeutic settings.</p></p>
