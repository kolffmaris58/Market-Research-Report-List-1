<p><p>The truck steel wheel market primarily comprises three types: casting, forging, and other methods. Casting involves pouring molten steel into molds to create wheels with intricate designs and shapes, offering cost efficiency for mass production. Forging, on the other hand, involves shaping steel through deformation, providing superior strength and durability, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. Other methods may include composite materials or hybrid approaches, catering to niche markets or enhancing specific performance characteristics. Each type serves distinct needs within the trucking industry.</p></p>
