<p><p>The civil drone market is primarily divided into two types: fixed wing drones and rotary wing drones. Fixed wing drones resemble traditional aircraft and are suited for long-range missions due to their aerodynamic design, offering extended flight times and efficiency. In contrast, rotary wing drones, such as quadcopters, are versatile and excel in vertical takeoff and landing, making them ideal for applications requiring flexibility and maneuverability, such as surveillance, deliveries, and aerial photography. Both types cater to diverse civil use cases.</p></p>
