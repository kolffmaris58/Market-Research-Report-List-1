<p><p>The Clean Power LED Drivers market primarily consists of two types: Constant Current and Regulated Current drivers. Constant Current drivers maintain a steady output current, ensuring consistent brightness and optimal performance of LEDs, making them ideal for applications requiring precise illumination levels. Regulated Current drivers, on the other hand, adjust their output based on varying input voltage to deliver a stable power supply, enhancing the efficiency and lifespan of lighting systems. Both types cater to diverse energy-efficient lighting solutions, promoting sustainability.</p></p>
