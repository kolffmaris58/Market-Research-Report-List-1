<p><p>The touchscreen chip market is primarily divided into single touch and multi-touch categories. Single touch technology allows only one point of contact on the screen at a time, suitable for basic applications like simple user interfaces. In contrast, multi-touch technology enables multiple points of contact simultaneously, enhancing interactivity and user experience. This enables gestures such as pinch-to-zoom and swipe actions, making it ideal for smartphones, tablets, and advanced user interfaces. The multi-touch segment is experiencing significant growth due to increasing consumer demand for intuitive interactions.</p></p>
